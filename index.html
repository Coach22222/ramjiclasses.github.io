About Us

At RamjiClasses, we are dedicated to providing expert English coaching to help students excel in their exams and enhance their language skills. Our experienced instructor, Harendra Vishwakarma, focuses on individual learning styles to foster a supportive and engaging environment.

We believe that every student has the potential to succeed. Our tailored curriculum, interactive classes, and extensive resources ensure that students gain the confidence they need to master English.

Under Harendra Sir’s guidance, students not only improve their academic performance but also develop a passion for the English language through engaging activities and practical applications.

Our Courses

Class 9 English

This course covers key topics in English literature, grammar, and comprehension. Students will engage in interactive sessions that enhance their understanding and skills.

Class 10 English

In this course, students will focus on advanced grammar, writing skills, and literature analysis. Our experienced teachers provide personalized support to ensure success.

Class 11 English

This course aims to deepen students’ knowledge of English through literature, poetry, and composition. Emphasis is placed on critical thinking and analysis.

Class 12 English

Our final course prepares students for their board examinations, focusing on literature, advanced grammar, and effective writing techniques. Practice tests and revision sessions are included.
